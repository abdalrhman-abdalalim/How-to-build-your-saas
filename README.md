# How-to-build-your-saas
> **SaaS** $\rightarrow$ Software as a Service.
> 
> _Note: The goal is to build a scalable, subscription-based solution._

## Phase 1: Foundation & Validation

### Step 1: Market Research

- **Target Audience:** Who is the customer?
    
- **Problem Discovery:** What is the specific pain point?
    
- **Competitive Analysis:** Who are the current players and what is their weakness?
    

### Step 2: Build Prototype

- **Core Value:** Show the essential value proposition immediately.
    
- **Feedback Loop:** Get real feedback from real users.
    
- **Feature Focus:** Focus on a **single core feature**; avoid over-engineering.
    
- **UX Design:** Focus on user flow and intuitive design.
    

### Step 3: Validation of Idea

- **Waitlist:** Build early hype and capture emails.
    
- **Early Adopters Program:** Create a closed group for initial testing and testimonials.
    

---

## Phase 2: Execution & Launch

### Step 4: Build MVP (Minimum Viable Product)

- **Core Feature:** Ship fast and iterate.
    
- **Development Mantra:** "If you aren't embarrassed by your first version, you launched too late."
    

### Step 5: Choose Your Pricing Model

|**Model**|**Type**|**Notes**|
|---|---|---|
|**Subscription**|Monthly/Yearly|Classic recurring revenue (e.g., Netflix).|
|**Freemium**|Free $\rightarrow$ Paid|Entry-level access to upsell later.|
|**Usage-Based**|Pay-as-you-go|Common for APIs/Tokens (e.g., OpenAI).|
|**Flat Rate**|One price/All features|Simple and transparent (e.g., B2B tools).|
|**Per User**|Seat-based|Scalable for teams (e.g., Slack, Notion).|

### Step 6: Find Your Market Strategy

- **Growth Channels:**
    
    - Partnerships & Paid Ads.
        
    - Free Trials & Educational Content (SEO).
        
    - Reviews & Social Proof.
        

---

## Phase 3: Scaling & Support

### Step 7: Build the Team

- Transition from solo founder to hiring for specialized roles (Customer Support, Engineering).
    

### Step 8: Customer Support

- Implement systems for feedback and troubleshooting to maintain high retention.
    

### Step 9: Scalability

- Ensure the technical architecture can handle growth (Frontend/Backend optimization).
    

### Step 10: Prepare for Challenges

- **Churn Rate:** Monitor how many users leave.
    
- **Competition:** Stay agile as new players enter.
    

### Step 11: Continuous Improvement

- **Iterate:** Always update and refine based on usage data.
    

---

## 📊 Key Metrics to Track (KPIs)

- **MRR:** Monthly Recurring Revenue.
    
- **ARR:** Annual Recurring Revenue.
    
- **LTV:** Lifetime Value of a customer.
    
- **CAC:** Customer Acquisition Cost.
